Tristan Tzara-Omorina nd Evropom

Tristan Tzara was a screamo / hardcore band from Dortmund, Germany. Their name is taken from founding dadaist Samy Rosenstock who used "Tristan Tzara" as his pseudonym. They have recorded two studio LP records before their ending in year 2002. the original lineup: Robert - vocal Ines - vocal Christian-guitar Miso - guitar Sven - bass guitar Thomas - drums (on Da Ne Zaboravis) Nils - drums (on Omorina Nad Evropom)
